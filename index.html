About Me

       I am a PhD student of the 2021 cohort at the Natural Language Processing Laboratory of Northeastern University, Shenyang, China, under the supervision of Professor Tong Xiao. My research focuses on the methods of deep reinforcement learning for sequence generation tasks, with a current emphasis on: 1) the training efficiency of sequence generation models based on reinforcement learning; 2) the construction and training of Reward models (with a preference for larger language models), as detailed in my papers. Additionally, I have participated in machine translation workshops such as WGT, QE, and WMT.
